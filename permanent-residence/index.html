Types of Applications

Your permanent home in a land of endless opportunity.

Express Entry

A Canadian Study Permit allows international students to attend designated institutions, offering world-class education, cultural exchange, and professional growth while building a strategic foundation for work permits and permanent residency.

Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)

An eTA allows visa-exempt travelers quick, affordable entry to Canada for tourism, study or for business. Valid for five years, it facilitates multiple short visits and provides a convenient pathway for international travelers.

Permanent Resident & Renewal

A Visitor TRV allows nationals from visa-required countries to enter Canada for tourism, family, or business. It offers first-hand experience and networking while providing flexible entry for up to six months.
