Entrepreneur / Investor Programs

The Entrepreneur and Investor Programs offer foreign business people a pathway to permanent residence by starting or investing in Canadian businesses. This route leverages capital and expertise to build successful enterprises.

Self-Employed

This program targets artists, athletes, and cultural professionals with significant self-employed experience. It allows talented individuals to immigrate by creating their own employment and contributing to Canada’s vibrant cultural or athletic landscape without needing a traditional job offer.

Startup Visa

The Start-Up Visa provides permanent residency to entrepreneurs securing support from designated Canadian investors. It targets innovative, scalable ventures capable of creating local jobs and competing on a global stage.
