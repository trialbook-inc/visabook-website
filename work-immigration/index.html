Types of Applications

Build your future in Canada’s thriving economy

Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA)

The LMIA program provides employer-backed work permits, offering valuable Canadian job experience. This pathway strengthens permanent residency applications through Express Entry, serving as a reliable route for building a long-term life.

Employer-Specific Work Permits

Employer-specific work permits authorize foreign nationals to work exclusively for one employer in a designated role. These permits often require a Labor Market Impact Assessment, providing a structured, secure employment pathway.

Spousal Open Work Permits

The Spousal Open Work Permit allows partners of international students or workers to work for any Canadian employer. This provides financial stability and work experience, while strengthening future permanent residency applications.
