Sponsorships

Family Sponsorship allows Canadian citizens and permanent residents to bring their loved ones to Canada indefinitely. This vital pathway supports national values by reuniting spouses, children, parents, and grandparents, granting them the legal right to live and thrive together.

Canada aims to welcome roughly 80,000+ family members every year.

Types of Applications

Your bridge to a lifetime of family moments

Parental Sponsorship

This program enables Canadian citizens and permanent residents to sponsor their parents and grandparents for permanent residency. It fosters multi-generational family unity, allowing elders to live indefinitely in Canada while accessing world-class healthcare and social benefits.

Spousal Sponsorship

Spousal Sponsorship enables Canadian citizens and permanent residents to sponsor their marriage or common-law partners for permanent residency. This pathway prioritizes family reunification, allowing couples to live together while the sponsored partner gains full rights to work and settle.

Dependent Sponsorship

Dependent Sponsorship allows Canadian citizens and permanent residents to bring their biological or adopted children to Canada permanently. This stream prioritizes family stability, providing children with immediate access to world-class education, universal healthcare, and a secure path to citizenship.
